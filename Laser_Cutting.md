# LASER CUTTING Journey
Laser cutting is a precision technology that uses a focused laser beam to cut or engrave materials such as metal, wood, and plastic. During my first experience using a laser cutter, I worked with Inkscape to create designs. Initially, it was challenging to familiarize myself with the terminology and tools in the software, but I quickly realized that practice would help me improve. Stepping up to the machine after only observing others was thrilling, as I could finally transform my ideas into tangible creations, experiencing the impressive accuracy and creativity that laser cutting offers.


# My Journey
 I initially thought that all the machines would be difficult to learn, but after gaining experience with laser cutting, I realized that it is much easier than I anticipated. It simply requires time to master their use effectively.
